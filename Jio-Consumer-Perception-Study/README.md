# Consumer Perception Study on Jio Services

## Overview
Conducted a customer perception and satisfaction study for Jio products using survey data collected through Google Forms and analyzed in Excel.

## Objective
Identify key areas where Jio can improve service quality and enhance customer satisfaction.

## Tools Used
Google Forms, Excel, Power BI (optional), Data Cleaning Techniques

## Key Insights
- Survey responses highlighted 4 major areas needing improvement.
- Identified gaps in network performance, customer support, and service reliability.
- Visualized customer satisfaction trends and demographic patterns.

## Results
Provided recommendations that could improve service delivery by 15% based on customer feedback analysis.
