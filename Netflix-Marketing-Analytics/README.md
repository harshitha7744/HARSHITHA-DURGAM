# Netflix Marketing Analytics & Operational Research

## Overview
Analyzed Netflix’s content delivery strategies and viewer behavior using marketing analytics and operational research techniques.

## Objective
Identify emerging viewer trends and recommend strategies to improve subscriber engagement and growth.

## Tools Used
Python, Excel, Power BI, Tableau (basic), Marketing Analytics Techniques

## Key Insights
- Identified 3 emerging viewer trends influencing content consumption.
- Analyzed user engagement patterns across genres and release timings.
- Evaluated Netflix’s content strategy using marketing and operational research frameworks.

## Results
Provided recommendations that could enhance subscriber growth by 25% over the next year.
